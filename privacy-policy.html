Privacy Policy

We recognise the importance of information privacy. This document describes what personal information we receive and collect when you use the site. We hope that this information will help you make informed decisions about the personal information you provide to us.

Publicly available information
If you just browse the site without registration, information about you is not published on the site.When you write or edit materials on the site, you publish every word of what you wrote, and this information will be stored and shown to other visitors of the project. This applies to articles, useful tips, materials, personal user pages, comments, etc.

Identification of visitors
You may or may not register on the site.If you register, you will be identified by your member name. This may be your real name (if you wish) or you may prefer to post under a pseudonym, which is the name you used when you created your account. Other registered visitors to the site will be able to see the details you provided when you registered.

E-mail
The email address you provide when you register is not shown to other visitors to the site.We may retain emails and other letters sent by users in order to process user questions, respond to enquiries, and improve our services.

Cookie
When you visit a website, one or more cookies are sent to your computer.When you register on a site, additional cookies may be sent to your computer to help you avoid re-entering your username (and possibly your password) the next time you visit. You can erase them at the end of your session if you are using a public computer and do not wish to reveal your pseudonym to subsequent users of the computer (in which case you should also clear your browser cache).We use cookies to improve the quality of our services by preserving user preferences and tracking trends in user behaviour, such as searches. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you may choose to refuse cookies completely or set up to show notifications when cookies are sent. However, without cookies, some features of the site may not work properly.

Logging
Each time you visit our website, our servers automatically record information that your browser transmits when you visit web pages. This information typically includes the requested web page, your computer's IP address, browser type, browser language settings, the date and time of the request, and one or more cookies that allow us to accurately identify your browser.

Links
On this site, links may be in a format that allows us to track whether visitors are using them.This information is used to improve the quality of our advertising.

Changes to the privacy policy
Please note that the privacy policy may change from time to time.Any changes to the privacy policy are posted on this page.
